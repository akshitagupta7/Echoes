"Give Me The Chance,I'll Show You The Magic"-Echoes

Conceptual restaurants serve more than just food on the platter. They serve a vision, an idea and garnish its uniqueness. Echoes is one such unique café which is a distinctive restaurant which is managed by deaf and mute staff and implements techniques which are not common in the industry. ECHOES makes it to the league of ideas which live duly upto its professional and social responsibility.

It’s not just the concept of Echoes which is unique, so is its food and ambience. It is a hub where you can relish the best of various Continental, Italian, Mexican, Chinese and American delicacies served with a fusion twist. With sumptuous food, a soul-comforting ambience and a unique concept; Echoes stands at the peak in the league of its own.

The goal of the team is to make ECHOES a café which is completely managed by deaf and mute staff and create an avenue where the specially ables staff can work with next level confidence. This goal will soon be attained as the walk towards the destiny has already begun. With Echoes recently opening in Bangalore, the expansion of the objective has begun. Soon, Echoes will be heard all around.

So far Echoes has made all the right sounds and has been accepted and appreciated all over the country with various media platforms spreading the word of goodness for Echoes. The best thing about the café is the amount of confidence it provides to its staff. It reforms the staff-customer relationship in a very different and positive way.
